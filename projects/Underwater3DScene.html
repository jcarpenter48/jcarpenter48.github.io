Underwater Programmatic 3D Scene

2023-03-07

This 3D Scene was written for CS 3451 during my time at Georgia Tech. The scene was drawn and renderred programmatically in Processing. The goal of the project was to demonstrate graphics programming principles, such as animation via matrix manipulation, manipulation of the stack, use of primitives, instantiation of defined models, and texture mapping.
